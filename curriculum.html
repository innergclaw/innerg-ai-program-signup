Weeks 1~4 · Getting Comfortable With AI
Week 1 May 4
AI Is Already Part of Your Life

What We'll Talk About

  • What AI actually is (and what it isn't)
  • How you're already using AI every day ~ in your phone, apps, even Netflix
  • Why understanding AI matters for you and your family
  • Icebreaker: "When did AI surprise you?"

Let's Try It Together

  • Open ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini for the first time
  • Say hi ~ introduce yourself to AI
  • Ask it something you're curious about, see what happens

Open Questions

"Is AI gonna take my job?" "Should I be scared?" All feelings welcome here.

Week 2 May 11
How AI Thinks (In Simple Terms)

What We'll Talk About

  • How AI learns ~ think of it like a super-powered autocomplete, not a brain
  • Why AI sometimes gets things wrong
  • The difference between AI, robots, and regular apps
  • Why it matters to know the difference

Let's Try It Together

  • Test AI's knowledge: ask it about your neighborhood
  • Can you catch it making something up?
  • Group game: "Spot the AI mistake"

Open Questions

"How do I know if I can trust it?" "Is it always right?"

Week 3 May 18
The Big Three ~ ChatGPT, Claude & Gemini

What We'll Talk About

  • What each one is good at
  • When to use which one
  • Free vs. paid ~ what you actually need
  • Finding your favorite

Let's Try It Together

  • Ask all three the same question
  • Compare how they answer ~ what's different?
  • Pick your favorite and tell us why

Open Questions

"Which one should I use?" "Is one smarter than the others?"

Week 4 May 25
Talking to AI ~ Getting Better Results

What We'll Talk About

  • Why the way you ask matters more than which tool you use
  • A simple formula: who you are → what you need → how you want it
  • Common mistakes everyone makes
  • How to get AI to give you exactly what you want

Let's Try It Together

  • Fix 5 bad questions and turn them into great ones
  • Test your improved questions live
  • Challenge: get the perfect answer on your first try

Open Questions

"Why didn't it give me what I wanted?" "Am I doing it wrong?"
